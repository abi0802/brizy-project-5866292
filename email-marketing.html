Spectrum Freight 2020

On this page I will be talking through what I created and what I learn’t about Email Marketing and Mail chimp as this was a completely new skill for me to learn during my apprenticeship.

The main goal for email marketing at Spectrum was;

To let our customer’s, staff and online community know our latest news on the company.To do this whilst keeping in line with our Going Paperless campaign by 2025.

To create this without using any money, as we didn’t have the biggest budget for marketing. I choose to do email marketing as it was the most, easiest, paperless and money efficent way to do so. It is also a nice way to move the company into the online world. For this I choose Mailchimp as my main tool to use as you can use a lot of features for free and without having to pay.I wanted to learn the best tool for the job.It is also recognisable by other companies.
